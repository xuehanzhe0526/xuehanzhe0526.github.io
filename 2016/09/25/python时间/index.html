The Time Module

Unix timestamps
时间戳：1970第一秒

  • a timestamp of 0.0 would represent the epoch, and a timestamp of 60.0 would represent one minute after the epoch。

Converting Timestamps

gmtime() function一种人们可以看懂的格式
返回一个 struct_time class.

  • m_year: The year of the timestamp
  • tm_mon: The month of the timestamp (1-12)
  • tm_mday: The day in the month of the timestamp (1-31)
  • tm_hour: The hour of the timestamp (0-23)
  • tm_min: The minute of the timestamp (0-59)

Coordinated Universal Time

UTC corresponds roughly to Greenwich Mean Time, except that it does not follow daylight saving time.

  • The time module deals primarily with timestamps in UTC.

Formatting Dates

datetime object 输出的格式 ：
2016-01-06 13:51:25.849719
虽然已经很清楚，但是我想可以更进一步改进：
这里用到 datetime class’s的一个方法strftime()

接受一个格式化的字符串（常用的"%"）作为它的输入。

  • (“%b”)Month as locale’s abbreviated name.
  • (“%d”)Day of the month as a zero-padded decimal number.
  • (“%Y”)Year with century as a decimal number.
